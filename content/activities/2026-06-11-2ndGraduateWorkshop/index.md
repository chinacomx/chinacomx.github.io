You are cordially invited to the 2nd ChinaComx Graduate Workshop "Practices and Politics of Lianhuanhua, Comics and Picture Books" taking place on Thursday, 18 June 2026, in HCTS Room 400.02.21.

Participants include incoming 2026 ChinaComx Visiting Scholars [Amelia Kin Wai Chu](https://research.flw.ugent.be/en/kinwai.chu) and [Chang Hasheminezhad-Li](https://ssu.elearning.unipd.it/mod/page/view.php?id=445439), special guests Di Yang (The University of Manchester) and Paul Zekun Chen (Heidelberg HCTS), as well as the whole [ChinaComx Team](https://chinacomx.github.io/team/).

We are happy to welcome you in the audience and during the Q&A sessions!
