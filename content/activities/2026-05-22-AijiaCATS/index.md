ChinaComx PhD Candidate [Aijia Zhang](https://chinacomx.github.io/team/aijia/) is to take part in this semester’s [CATS](https://www.cats.uni-heidelberg.de/) Forum, “New Projects, New Initiatives, Work-in-Progress,” held this Friday, __22 May 2026__, __from 2-5 PM at HCTS__, __Room 212__. The forum seeks to promote CATS’ interdisciplinary intellectual exchange, mutual inspiration, and knowledge generation. It brings together scholars working across Asian regions and disciplines to share ongoing and emerging research projects.

Aijia will present an ongoing chapter on the materiality of _lianhuanhua_ titled __Lianhuanhua in Practice: From Visual Form to Material Networks__. Her presentation will begin at __15:15__. Read more about Aijia’s chapter in the abstract: 
> In this chapter, I examine the materiality of lianhuanhua as a medium, practice, and cultural product. Integrating insights from Hannah Miodrag and Bruno Latour, I conceptualize lianhuanhua as a material-semiotic network in which meaning and cultural knowledge are produced. Drawing on a large corpus of metadata and critical essays, I employ computational methods to map and visualize networks of production and circulation, revealing how lianhuanhua meaning is assembled, stabilized, and transformed across contexts.
