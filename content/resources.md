  {{< accordionItem title="THE CHINACOMX LIANHUANHUA BIBLIOGRAPHY, v 1.5." icon="book" md=false >}}
  
  <p class="mb-4">We are happy to share our comprehensive reference list of selected scholarly publications on lianhuanhua—Chinese comics—compiled by the ChinaComx project (most recent update: 10 April 2025).</p> 

  <p class="mb-4">This list is to serve as both an entry point for students of Chinese visual culture and a curated snapshot of current scholarship. It is not a peer-reviewed bibliography, nor should exclusion from this list be taken as a negative judgment; it is simply a selection of texts we find particularly interesting at this moment and hope will be useful to other researchers.</p> 

  <p class="mb-6">We hope you might approach this list as an invitation: it invites you to read and engage with the vast academic literature on all things lianhuanhua; it invites you to share with us other publications that we might have overlooked; and it invites all of us to explore the fascinating world(s) of Chinese comics.</p>
