ChinaComx PI [Lena Henningsen](https://chinacomx.github.io/team/lena/) presented the work of the project and her own research at the University of Mainz/Germersheim last week. In her presentation for colleagues and students from the [Department of Translation at Uni Mainz](https://fb06.uni-mainz.de/), Lena gave an overview of the ChinaComx project and why _lianhuanhua_ were so popular in China. Her talk is titled “__Unterhaltung, Bildung und Propaganda im Comic? Geschichte und Entwicklung der chinesischen _Lianhuanhua_ 连环画__”.

To read Lena’s recent research on Lu Xun and _lianhuanhua_, please click [here](https://www.closure.uni-kiel.de/closure11/henningsen); to learn more about the ChinaComx project, please click [here](https://chinacomx.github.io/about/).
